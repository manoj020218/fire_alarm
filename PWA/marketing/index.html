A fire pump that fails silently is worse than no pump at all.

Most fire systems are only checked manually, occasionally. A tripped pump, a dropped pressure line, a low water tank or a dead DG battery can go unnoticed for weeks — until the moment it matters. FireGuard watches everything, all the time, and tells you the instant it changes.

Real-time monitoring

Live status of pumps, pressure, tank levels, fuel, battery and panels — updated every few seconds on a clean dashboard and mobile app.

Instant SMS + app alerts

Critical alarms reach your team immediately — by push and by SMS over the gateway's own cellular link, even if the internet is down.

Triple-redundant uplink

4G, Ethernet and Wi-Fi with automatic failover — so your fire system stays connected even when one path fails.

Frequently asked

What exactly is billed "per device"?

Each piece of equipment you connect that can raise an alarm — a pump, a pressure sensor, a tank, a panel — counts as one monitored device. You only pay for what you actually monitor, and only after your 3-month free trial.

Do I need internet at the site?

The gateway works over 4G (SIM), Ethernet or Wi-Fi and automatically fails over between them. Critical SMS alerts go out over cellular even if broadband is down.

What if the connection drops?

Readings are buffered on the gateway and back-filled to the cloud when it reconnects, so you never lose history.

Will it work with my existing fire equipment?

If your pumps, sensors and panels support RS485 / Modbus (most modern fire equipment does), FireGuard connects to them. We help you map them during onboarding.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Access is role-based and authenticated, data is stored securely, and firmware is updated over secure OTA with automatic rollback protection.
