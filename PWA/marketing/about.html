Who we are

About FireGuard

FireGuard is a smart monitoring platform built by IoT Soft to bring real-time visibility, logging, and instant alerts to critical equipment — starting with fire-fighting systems. We help facility owners, maintenance teams, and integrators know the health of their pumps, pressure lines, water tanks, and panels 24×7, from anywhere, on the web and on mobile.

What we do

We combine rugged internet-connected gateways with a modern cloud dashboard and mobile app. Sensors and equipment connect to a FireGuard gateway on site; the gateway securely streams live status to the cloud, where our platform records history, detects abnormal conditions, and sends alerts by SMS, email, and push notification — so problems are caught before they become emergencies.

Where it's used

  • Fire-fighting pump rooms, sprinkler and hydrant systems, and water storage.
  • Farmhouse and agricultural pump control.
  • Energy and utility metering.
  • Intrusion and equipment monitoring for large solar plants and remote sites.
  • Custom monitoring solutions built around a specific problem — talk to us about yours.
